Introduction

Publisher insights is aimes to provide real time information about your viewer's consumption habits.

It consists of 3 tabs Trending, Engagement and Content

Data is provided on an hourly basis and is updated every minute

Navigating to Publisher insights

  1. Log in to your Video Cloud account.
  2. Cick on Analytics
  3. On the left navigation menu Click Insights
If you would like publisher insights enabled for your account please contact your customer sucess manager.

Trending

You can select your concurrent viewers by VOD, Live or All

Engagement

The engagement data helps understand how subscribers use the service and will provide a baseline success rate for encouraging customers to keep coming back. Engagement insights reveal the audience's interaction with the library. Detailed video consumption trends, average days streamed, and activity over time.

Content

The content data is essential for identifying the most popular content among subscribers and gaining insights into current trends. It enables a detailed analysis of audience interaction, featuring a content explorer and a library breakdown for popular series.
