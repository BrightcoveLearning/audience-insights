Introduction

Audience insight data integrates your OTT (Over-the-Top) video data sources with subscription video insights to provide actionable insights based on key industry benchmarks.

While video content is still king, consumers' perceived value will reduce subscriber churn. How do you give subscribers the feeling that they always have something new and relevant for their interests? The answer is to make your content library work harder to offer value. This requires that you need a deep understanding of how your content assets fit together. Data analytics hold the key.

Your Audience Insight data includes:

Unique insights

Increase audience lifetime value with unique audience insights for video businesses.

Data-driven results

Improve audience aquisition, service engagement, and reduce churn.

Customer success

Brightcove provides ideas and recommendations based on industry best practices.

At-risk subscriber identification

Churn prediction uses machine learning to identify subscribers who are at-risk of leaving your video service.

Scorecard

The Audience Insights scorecard provides export functionality making it easy to share action-oriented tasks with your teams. These reports support a positive ROI on your video business by making it easy for your teams to use highly contextual data in popular marketing and CRM platforms.
