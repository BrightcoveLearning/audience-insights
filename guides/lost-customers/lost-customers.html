Introduction

The lost customers section helps keep track of loose customers. In this order, this section provides information to the company that helps analyze the factors that favor these losses.

From your audience insight scorecard, select the Lost Customers tab to see the information.

Lost customers in the last

On the Lost Customers section, click the month button to select the specific date for your reporting data.

By clicking the following tabs the different options to retrieve data will be displayed:

  • Rolling
  • To date
  • Last complete

Lost customers per day

Lost customer metrics will help gain insights into your marketing effectiveness.

Overview

Quickly view a summary of lost customers data per day.

Review lost customers data:

  • Total - Total number of subscribers who have canceled their plan
  • Involuntary - Typically catches subscribers with a declined or canceled credit card
  • Voluntary - Includes an active action by the customer to unsubscribe
  • Lost trials - Lost customers who didn't subscribe after a free trial period

Actions

  • Click to expand the graph and see more information.
  • Click to download data as a CSV file or a PNG image.
  • Select to see lost customers displayed in numbers or percentages
  • Select a date range for your data.
  • Select the x-axis granularity to be Day, Week or Month.
  • Check to automatically change the x-axis dimension to day, week or month.

Key metrics

Review key metrics data:

  • Paid churn percent - Percentage of your paid subscription base that has left the service in the past 30 days
  • Lost trials percent - Percentage of lost customers who didn't subscride after a free trial period in the past 30 days
  • Percent stalled trialists - Percentage of Stalled Trialists. Some people sign up, watch a video, and never participate again
  • Percent stalled users - Percentage of inactive subscribers

Actions

  • Click to show benchmark data.
  • Click to download data as a CSV file or a PNG image.

Milestones

Milestones track progress against your decisions and are persistent throughout each tab of the Scorecard.

Actions

  • Click to add a new milestone.
  • Click to select which views to show.

Scheduled cancellations

Scheduled cancellations describes customers who have marked their subscription to expire at the end of their current paid term. We count this upcoming voluntary churn based on the last day they can use the service.

Actions

  • Click to download data as PNG image or PDF.

Lost customers by

Lost customer by allows you to filter lost subscribers by different options.

Actions

  • Click to download data as PNG image or PDF.
