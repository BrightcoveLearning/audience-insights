Introduction

Engagement hours viewed by allows you to quickly determine the engagement of the audience with the service provided. With this information, it's possible to obtain data related to the hours viewed by plan, channel, device, and viewing location.

Hours viewed by

Hours viewed by metrics allows you to see the hours viewed by plan, channel, device, and viewing location.

On the hours viewed by section, click the plan button to select an specific option for your reporting data.

By clicking the following options, the different graphics will be displayed:

  • Plan

  • Channel

  • Device

  • Viewing location

Actions

Here are the actions that are available for this graph.

  • Click to download data as PNG image or PDF.

  • Click to zoom in or out the graphic.

Average hours viewed per week by

The average hours viewed per week by metrics provide and unified view of the hours viewed per user by plan, device, channel, and location with country or region.

Actions

Here are the actions that are available for the average hours viewed per week by section.

  • Click to show the data by plan, device, channel, and location with country or region.

  • Click to display more information.
